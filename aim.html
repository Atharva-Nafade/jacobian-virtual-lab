Experiment Aim

Jacobian of Matrix

Aim of the Experiment

To understand the concept of the Jacobian of a matrix (Jacobian determinant of a system of functions) and to learn how to compute it for transformations involving two and three variables.

Objectives

  • Define the Jacobian matrix and the Jacobian determinant for a system of functions.
  • Compute the Jacobian of two functions in two variables.
  • Compute the Jacobian of three functions in three variables.
  • Understand the geometric interpretation of the Jacobian as a scaling factor in coordinate transformations.
  • Apply Jacobians in change-of-variable problems in multiple integrals.
  • Verify the property J × J' = 1 for inverse transformations.

Prerequisites

  • Partial differentiation of functions of two or more variables.
  • Determinants of 2×2 and 3×3 matrices.
  • Basic concept of functions and their domains.
